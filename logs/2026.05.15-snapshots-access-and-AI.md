# Lessons Learned

* Snapshots transform experimentation into reversible operations.
* Group-based permissions scale better than direct user permissions.
* Explicit deny permissions override inherited allows.
* Event Viewer exposes operating systems as ecosystems of service identities.
* Agent frameworks should initially be sandboxed with least privilege.
* Local AI agents can perform meaningful filesystem and scripting tasks.
* Process cleanup and service orchestration are critical when working with long-running agents.
* AI-assisted development benefits from iterative human validation.
* Separating experimental AI systems from core infrastructure supports safer development and clearer architectural boundaries.
